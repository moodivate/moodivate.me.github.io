About The Creators: Marian Abuhazi and Paula Peñaranda

Marian Abuhazi

Marian Abuhazi was born on April 10, 2002 in Caracas, Venezuela. She is a rising junior in Miami Beach Senior High School and is a girl of many aspirations. She is very participative in school activities as she is first chair viola in the orchestra as well as Vice President of that club. She is active in the Social Studies Honor Society where she competes in history bowls, volunteers at Mount Sinai Medical Center during the summer, is he Vice President of the SHAPE club to give back to the community, and most recently became a student at Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. Marian is very interested in the STEM fields and desires to study medicine at a top university. Knowledge of Computer Science obtained from Girls Who Code will prepare her for any career in STEM, even if she does not pursue a Medical degree. She is the co-creator of MOODivate and is extremely excited for the internet to try out her idea.

Paula Peñaranda

Paula Catalina Peñaranda was born in Miami, Florida in 2001 and is of Colombian descent. As a young girl, she always enjoyed reading and solving puzzles. When she was 4 she moved to Colombia for a year and learned to speak Spanish. At the age of five, she learned how to speak English and found a passion in cooking. Paula is a very dedicated student and holds a leadership position in her schools NJROTC program and is a member of her Schools Born Brave club, a club whose main focus is acceptance. She has earned her AICE diploma, a certification from Cambridge University and she has been awarded an AP Scholar award. She enjoys volunteering and helping those in need because she knows the world can become a better place. She has attended summer programs such as the National Student Leadership Conference and Girls Who Code. Paula is also the co-creator of MOODivate. She is an rising senior and hopes to become a Lawyer in the future in order to continue with her passion of helping those less fortunate than her.
